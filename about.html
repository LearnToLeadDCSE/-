About Learn to Lead

Dedicated Students Making a Difference

Who We Are

Learn to Lead is a student group from UET Peshawar working on a university project focused on greenery and cleanliness. Our goal is to promote a clean, green, and healthy environment on campus and in the community.

We create awareness by sharing informative videos, images, and articles through our website and social media platforms. Our team is actively involved in promoting plantation, encouraging responsible waste disposal, and spreading a positive environmental mindset among students.

With a team of nine dedicated members, Learn to Lead aims to educate, inspire, and motivate people to contribute towards a cleaner and greener future. We believe that small steps taken today can create a big impact tomorrow.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable and eco-friendly campus environment that inspires future generations to value and protect our planet.

Our Goal

Promote cleanliness and greenery through consistent action, education, and community engagement across UET Peshawar.

Our Values

Commitment, responsibility, teamwork, and a deep respect for the environment guide everything we do.
